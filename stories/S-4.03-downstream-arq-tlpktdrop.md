# S-4.03: Downstream ARQ with Piggybacked ACK/SACK and TLPKTDROP

> **Execute:** `/vsdd-factory:deliver-story S-4.03`

## Narrative

- **As a** console
- **I want to** have reliable downstream delivery with ACK/SACK piggybacked on upstream frames, and have overdue frames terminated by TLPKTDROP
- **So that** the terminal display reflects accurate session state even under lossy paths, and degraded connections surface actionable signals

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-001 (traces to BC-2.02.005 postcondition 1)
`ARQ.OnAck(ack_seq, sack_bitmap)` marks delivered frames as acknowledged and never delivers the same frame twice.
- **Test:** `TestARQ_OnAck_NoDuplicateDelivery`

### AC-002 (traces to BC-2.02.005 postcondition 2)
`ARQ.OnAck` delivers frames in sequence order. Frames received out of order are buffered until preceding gaps are filled.
- **Test:** `TestARQ_InOrderDelivery`

### AC-003 (traces to BC-2.02.005 postcondition 3)
The SACK bitmap in upstream frames is carried in the channel header (not outer header payload). ARQ reads SACK from channel header bytes.
- **Test:** `TestARQ_SACKInChannelHeader`

### AC-004 (traces to BC-2.02.006 postcondition 1)
`ARQ.TLPKTDROP(overdue_seq)` terminates the overdue frame: removes it from the retransmit queue and signals a degradation event to the metrics layer.
- **Test:** `TestARQ_TLPKTDROP_TerminatesOverdueFrame`

### AC-005 (traces to BC-2.02.006 postcondition 2)
TLPKTDROP fires only for frames that have exceeded their deadline (configurable timeout); frames in flight but not yet overdue are not dropped.
- **Test:** `TestARQ_TLPKTDROP_OnlyOverdueFrames`
