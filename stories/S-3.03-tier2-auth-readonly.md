# S-3.03: Tier-2 Per-Session Authorization and Read-Only Enforcement

> **Execute:** `/vsdd-factory:deliver-story S-3.03`

## Narrative

- **As an** access node
- **I want to** enforce per-session Tier 2 authorization and reject upstream keystrokes from read-only consoles
- **So that** session access is controlled at the granularity of individual sessions, not just SVTN membership

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-001 (traces to BC-2.05.003 postcondition 1)
`SessionAuth.Authorize(console_key, session_name)` succeeds for keys with console or control role registered for the session and returns `E-ADM-005` for unregistered keys.
- **Test:** `TestSessionAuth_AuthorizesRegisteredKey`

### AC-002 (traces to BC-2.05.003 postcondition 2)
Tier 2 authorization is enforced at the access node; the router does not perform session-level auth (router only sees SVTN admission).
- **Test:** `TestSessionAuth_RouterDoesNotEnforce`

### AC-003 (traces to BC-2.04.005 postcondition 1)
A read-only console can attach and receive downstream frames. Any upstream keystroke frame from a read-only console is rejected by the access node with `E-SES-003`.
- **Test:** `TestReadOnlyConsole_UpstreamRejected`

### AC-004 (traces to BC-2.04.005 postcondition 2)
A read-only console's upstream rejection does not affect other consoles' ability to send keystrokes.
- **Test:** `TestReadOnlyConsole_DoesNotAffectOtherConsoles`
